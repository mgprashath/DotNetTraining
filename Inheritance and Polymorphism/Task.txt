Task 09/30/2025: (Inheritance with abstract class)
------------------
Calculate employee Bonus
	1. If it was manager 10% as bonus on their salary
	2. If it was associate 5% as bonus on their salary
 
Write Abstract class for Employee and Make Manager and Associates as child. 
you can give two employee names and their designation and salary as constants in your main class.
 
Sample output:
	EmployeeName: Karthik , Designation: Associate , Salary: 10000 Bonus: 500, NetSalary: 10500
	EmployeeName: Harish , Designation: Manager , Salary: 20000 Bonus: 2000, NetSalary: 22000
